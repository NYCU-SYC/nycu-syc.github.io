Projects

Selected Projects

A broader view of research, translational, mentoring, and innovation-related projects drawn from the current CV and project history.

MRI + VLM

Brain Tumor Clinical Report Generation System

A clinically oriented system that combines deep learning-based expert models and a Vision-Language Model (VLM) to automatically generate complete and clinically meaningful brain tumor reports from MRI. The project integrates lesion segmentation, quantitative analysis, structured clinical outputs, and report drafting into a single end-to-end workflow.

3D Medical AI

Lesion-Sensitive Brain Tumor MRI Analysis

Research on lesion-sensitive 3D MRI analysis for brain tumor segmentation, candidate refinement, and quantitative evaluation. The work emphasizes clinically grounded metrics such as lesion-wise sensitivity, precision, F1, false positives per scan, and deployment-oriented robustness analysis.

Trustworthy Medical AI

Non-Contrast Brain Tumor Inference and Knowledge Distillation

A research direction investigating knowledge distillation and non-contrast inference for brain tumor analysis, with the goal of preserving clinically meaningful lesion detection performance while reducing reliance on contrast-enhanced imaging during inference.

CTO / Translational AI

NOVATERA: ICF Smart AI Rehabilitation System

As CTO, led the technical strategy and product development of an AI-assisted rehabilitation platform for home and clinical use. The project focused on functional assessment, interactive rehabilitation workflows, prototype design, and translation-oriented technical planning, and was recognized as a success-case project by Taiwan Ministry of Education while also advancing to the semifinal round of FITI.

Champion Project

AOCR AI Challenge: Acute Appendicitis CT Segmentation

Led a research team to develop a 3D CT-based segmentation framework for acute appendicitis in clinically complex cases. The project focused on lesion-sensitive detection, localization, segmentation, and robust system evaluation, and ultimately achieved Champion in the AOCR AI Challenge.

Mentorship / Healthcare Robotics

Medication Delivery Robot (StanCode Mentoring Project)

Mentored a student team developing a smart medication delivery robot that combines computer vision, sensor fusion, autonomous navigation, and dispensing. Guidance covered system design, technical framing, evaluation planning, and practical healthcare-oriented implementation.

Mentorship / Healthcare AI

Medication Image Recognition + RAG + LLM Platform (StanCode Mentoring Project)

Mentored a student team building a medication image recognition and education platform based on Detection/Classification + Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) + LLM. The system aimed to provide medication identification, grounded medical education responses, citation-backed answers, and safety-aware output design.

Omniguider Inc.

Diffusion-Based Image Synthesis and Generative Vision Applications

Contributed to diffusion-based image synthesis workflows and generative vision applications, with emphasis on image generation pipelines and model development for practical visual AI scenarios.

NCSIST / UAV Vision

GAN-Based Target Image Synthesis and RGB-to-Infrared Translation

Worked on GAN-based target image generation and RGB-to-infrared target image translation for aerial and defense-related computer vision applications. The generated imagery was used to expand limited datasets, improve downstream detection robustness, and support deployment of trained detection models on UAV platforms for real-world testing.

Translational Medical AI

AItewan DeepBT Detector–Plus

Co-developed a translational brain tumor AI system that achieved TFDA medical device approval, with U.S. FDA application pending. This project highlights experience in clinical translation, product-oriented AI development, and regulatory-facing medical AI implementation.

Independent Development

AItewan DeepBT Detector–A Plus

Independently developed a brain tumor AI system currently under TFDA and U.S. FDA medical device application review, reflecting ongoing work in translational innovation and clinically deployable medical AI product development.
