Research, Translational, and Innovation Projects

Expanded from the CV into a fuller project portfolio suitable for a GitHub Pages academic website.

NYCU Research

Brain Tumor SRS Clinical AI System

End-to-end clinical AI research for brain tumor stereotactic radiosurgery using an 11,000+ multicenter MRI dataset. The work includes DBI-MambaUNet, SA-FTL, small-lesion detection optimization, contrast-free analysis with GANs and knowledge distillation, structured clinical report generation with constrained VLMs, and DICOM SEG/SR standardization for PACS-compatible workflows.

CTO / Translational AI

NOVATERA: ICF Smart AI Rehabilitation System

AI-assisted rehabilitation platform for home and clinical environments. Responsibilities include technical strategy, system architecture, AI workflow design, prototype development, and translational deployment planning. The project was recognized as a success case by the Taiwan Ministry of Education and advanced to the FITI semifinals.

Mentorship

StanCode Healthcare AI Mentoring

Technical mentorship for student teams building healthcare AI systems, including a smart medication delivery robot and an educational medication recognition app using image detection/classification, RAG, and LLMs. Focus areas included system architecture, evaluation design, grounded medical QA, and safety-oriented response design.

Challenge Project

AOCR AI Challenge: Acute Appendicitis CT Segmentation

3D CT-based segmentation framework for acute appendicitis, designed for sensitive detection, localization, and segmentation in clinically complex cases. The project led to a championship result in the AOCR Kaggle AI Challenge and contributed to downstream awards, patent activity, and translational recognition.

Industry R&D

Omniguider Interactive Generative Imaging System

Interactive image system for the National Museum of Natural Science. The system used ControlNet for controllable diffusion guidance, LoRA for exhibit-specific adaptation, and diffusion-based image generation integrated with face-swapping techniques to improve realism and interaction quality. The project won the Excellence Award in the AI Application Challenge hosted by Taiwan’s Ministry of Digital Affairs.

Defense Computer Vision

NCSIST Drone Vision and Cross-Modal Generation

R&D on data-efficient drone vision using cGANs, knowledge distillation, and RGB-to-infrared image translation. The work aimed to address data scarcity and hardware constraints for deployment, while expanding synthetic training data for downstream detection and segmentation systems.

Translational Medical AI

AItewan DeepBT Detector–Plus

Co-developed translational brain tumor AI product with TFDA medical device approval obtained and U.S. FDA application pending.

Independent Translational Development

AItewan DeepBT Detector–A Plus

Independently developed brain tumor AI product with TFDA and U.S. FDA medical device applications pending.
