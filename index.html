Research Profile

Computer vision and multimodal AI for brain tumor analysis and clinically grounded report generation.

I work at the intersection of computer vision, deep learning, medical image analysis, multimodal learning, generative modeling, and vision-language systems. My recent work focuses on lesion-sensitive brain tumor MRI analysis, contrast-free inference, PACS-compatible deployment, and structured clinical report generation.

Research Themes

Brain Tumor MRI Analysis

Lesion-sensitive segmentation, candidate refinement, quantitative analysis, and small-lesion performance for stereotactic radiosurgery workflows.

Clinical Report Generation

Structured report generation from MRI by integrating expert deep learning models with constrained vision-language systems.

Trustworthy Medical AI

Knowledge distillation, contrast-free inference, PACS deployment, DICOM-compatible outputs, and evaluation centered on lesion-wise clinical metrics.
